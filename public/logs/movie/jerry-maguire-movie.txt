**Jerry Maguire (1996)** is an American romantic comedy-drama film written, produced, and directed by Cameron Crowe. The film stars Tom Cruise as the titular character, a high-powered sports agent who has a moral epiphany and writes a "mission statement" about the dishonesty in his industry.
