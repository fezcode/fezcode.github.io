Daughter / Patient Zero

A twelve-year-old girl and the first human to manifest the 'Touch of Rose'. Scientist / Strategist

A brilliant strategist who secretly used Proxanian Seeds and Sansu Syrup to develop her formula. She sought to create a botanical neural network but never intended to create a pathogen. After demanding to see Patient Zero (Pellera Orlong), she realized Willare's mistake involved her secret Proxanian materials. She remains silent about the source to protect her own life and research.
