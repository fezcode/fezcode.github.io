## About

**Meet Joe Black (1998)** is an American romantic fantasy drama film directed by Martin Brest. The film stars Brad Pitt as Death, who takes the form of a young man named Joe Black, and Anthony Hopkins as William Parrish, a wealthy media tycoon. On the eve of his 65th birthday, Parrish is visited by Death, who offers him more time on Earth in exchange for a guided tour of human life.

As Joe Black experiences the world, he becomes fascinated by human emotions and develops a romantic connection with Parrish's daughter, Susan (played by Claire Forlani). The film explores themes of life, death, love, and legacy, set against the backdrop of corporate intrigue within Parrish's company. It is known for its measured pacing, emotional performances, and the interplay between its supernatural premise and human drama.
