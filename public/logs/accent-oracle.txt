## About

Accent Oracle is an innovative website that uses advanced technology to detect and analyze accents. This tool is invaluable for individuals looking to understand their accent patterns, improve pronunciation in a new language, or simply explore the nuances of spoken language. It provides a unique and engaging way to interact with linguistic diversity.
