## Kingdom Two Crowns: Norse Lands Soundtrack (Extended): A Majestic Soundscape

![Album Cover](/images/logs/music/kingdom-two-crowns-norse-lands-soundtrack-extended-cover.png)

The official soundtrack for the **"Norse Lands"** DLC of the critically acclaimed strategy game, **Kingdom Two Crowns.**
Composed by **Kalandra**, this extended soundtrack immerses listeners in the harsh yet beautiful world of Norse mythology,
blending traditional Nordic folk instruments with modern orchestral arrangements. Each track evokes the epic scale of the game,
from the serene exploration of new lands to the intense battles against the Greed. It's a journey through captivating soundscapes
that perfectly complement the game's atmospheric pixel art and strategic gameplay.

## Trivia

*   **Game Connection:** The soundtrack is an integral part of "Kingdom Two Crowns: Norse Lands," a significant expansion to the popular micro-strategy game.
*   **Nordic Influence:** The score heavily features instruments and melodies inspired by Norse culture, enhancing the thematic depth of the "Norse Lands" expansion.
