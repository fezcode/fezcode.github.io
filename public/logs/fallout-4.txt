- **Date:** 2023-05-05

- **Link:** [Fallout 4 Official Website](https://fallout.bethesda.net/games/fallout-4)

## About

**Fallout 4** is an action role-playing game developed by Bethesda Game Studios and published by Bethesda Softworks. Released in November 2015, it is the fifth major installment in the *Fallout* series. The game is set in a post-apocalyptic Boston, Massachusetts, and the surrounding Massachusetts Commonwealth, approximately 210 years after a devastating nuclear war.

Players begin as the Sole Survivor of Vault 111, emerging from cryogenic stasis to a world irrevocably changed. Their quest is to find their abducted son, Shaun, and seek revenge for the murder of their spouse. Along the way, players explore a vast open world, encountering various factions, mutated creatures, and remnants of humanity struggling to survive in the wasteland.

*Fallout 4* introduces several new gameplay mechanics to the series, most notably a comprehensive settlement-building system, allowing players to construct and manage their own communities. The game features real-time combat with VATS (Vault-Tec Assisted Targeting System) returning, offering strategic targeting in slow motion. Character progression is handled through a SPECIAL (Strength, Perception, Endurance, Charisma, Intelligence, Agility, Luck) system and an extensive perk chart. The game emphasizes exploration, scavenging, crafting, and player choice, with numerous quests and emergent storytelling opportunities.

## My Thoughts

*Fallout 4* is a truly massive and ambitious open-world RPG, offering hundreds of hours of exploration and content in a wonderfully detailed post-apocalyptic setting. The Commonwealth is a desolate yet fascinating place, full of memorable locations, dangerous creatures, and tragic stories waiting to be uncovered. Bethesda's signature world-building shines through, creating a palpable sense of history and atmosphere.

The settlement-building system, while initially overwhelming, proved to be a surprisingly addictive addition. There's a deep satisfaction in turning a ruined plot of land into a thriving community, and the crafting system allows for extensive weapon and armor customization. The companion system is also a highlight, with many unique and well-written characters to accompany you on your journey, each with their own backstories and quests.

However, the game isn't without its drawbacks. The main story, while engaging enough, sometimes feels secondary to the sheer breadth of side content, and the dialogue system can feel a bit restrictive compared to previous *Fallout* titles. Performance issues, particularly on consoles at launch, were also a concern. Despite these minor criticisms, *Fallout 4* remains a highly enjoyable and immersive experience. It's a game that allows you to truly get lost in its world, defining your own adventure in the nuclear wasteland.
