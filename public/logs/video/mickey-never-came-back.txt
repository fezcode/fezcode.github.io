## About

**Mickey Never Came Back** is a dark and atmospheric animation by Ethereal Snake. It presents a haunting reimagining of Mickey Mouse as a traumatized veteran of the Vietnam War. The video is praised for its unique art style, voice acting, and its stark departure from the traditional Disney aesthetic, exploring themes of PTSD and the loss of innocence.
