# Mafia II

Mafia II is more than just an open-world action game; it's a cinematic journey through the Golden Era of America. Following the story of Vito Scaletta, a war veteran who returns home to Empire Bay, the game explores themes of loyalty, betrayal, and the cost of the American Dream.

While the world isn't as "busy" as a typical modern open-world game, its focus on narrative and atmosphere makes it one of the most compelling stories in the genre.
