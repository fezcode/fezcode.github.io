## About

**Halt and Catch Fire (2014)** is an American period drama television series created by Christopher Cantwell and Christopher C. Rogers. The series chronicles the personal computing boom of the 1980s and the subsequent rise of the World Wide Web in the 1990s. It follows four main characters: visionary Joe MacMillan, brilliant but troubled engineer Gordon Clark, ambitious software engineer Donna Clark, and rebellious coding prodigy Cameron Howe. Together and apart, they navigate the rapidly shifting technological landscape, striving to innovate, compete, and leave their mark on an industry that is constantly reinventing itself.

The show is set against the backdrop of significant technological advancements and cultural shifts, exploring the human stories behind the digital revolution. It delves into themes of ambition, failure, partnership, gender dynamics in tech, and the relentless pursuit of an idea. *Halt and Catch Fire* gained critical acclaim over its four seasons for its strong writing, deep character development, accurate historical detail, and its nuanced portrayal of the joys and heartbreaks of innovation.
