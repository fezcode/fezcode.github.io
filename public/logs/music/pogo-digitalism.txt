# Pogo - Digitalism

"Pogo" is perhaps the most iconic track from Digitalism, the German duo consisting of Jens "Jence" Moelle and İsmail "Isi" Tüfekçi. Released in 2007 on their debut album *Idealism*, the song perfectly captures the energy of the blog house era, blending punk-rock energy with polished electronic production.

## Trivia

- **Video Game Legend:** "Pogo" gained massive popularity through its inclusion in high-profile video games, most notably *FIFA 08* and *Need for Speed: ProStreet*. For many, it's the definitive "FIFA song" of its era.
- **Vocal Duty:** The vocals on the track were performed by Jens Moelle himself.
- **Hamburg Roots:** The duo formed in Hamburg back in 2004, initially meeting in a record store.
- **Timeless Appeal:** Even 15 years after its release, it was included in the *FIFA 23* World Cup update, proving its lasting impact on football culture and electronic music.
