## Overview

The complete original soundtrack for the legendary 2009 Flash game **Stick War**, composed by talented musicians **Chris Haigh**, **Waterflame**, and **Denny Schneidemesser**. These tracks perfectly captured the epic, sometimes melancholic, and always intense atmosphere of the struggle for Inamorta.

7. **Trumpet of Inamorta** (13:21)

Each composer brought a unique flavor to the game. Denny Schneidemesser's "Entering the Stronghold" is grand and cinematic, while Waterflame's "Field of Memories" provides a beautiful, reflective pause. Chris Haigh's tracks like "Battle of the Shadow Elves" and "Gates of Hell" are the heartbeat of the game's combat, driving the player forward with intense orchestrations.
