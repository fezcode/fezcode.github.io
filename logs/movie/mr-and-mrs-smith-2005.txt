Mr. & Mrs. Smith (2005) is a high-octane blend of action, romance, and comedy directed by Doug Liman. The film stars Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie as John and Jane Smith, a seemingly ordinary suburban couple whose marriage has grown stale. However, their lives are turned upside down when they discover that both are secret assassins working for rival agencies.

When they are assigned to eliminate the same target, their secret identities are revealed, leading to a deadly game of cat and mouse within their own home. The film is celebrated for the undeniable chemistry between its leads, its stylish action sequences, and its humorous take on domestic life through the lens of international espionage.

It's a quintessential popcorn movie that balances its explosive set pieces with witty dialogue and a playful deconstruction of marital tropes.
