## KIDS SEE GHOSTS: A Melancholic Masterpiece

![Album Cover](/images/logs/music/kids-see-ghosts-cover.png)

> AKASTIFIDALUH, that is how `Feel The Love` starts. I saw that in the comments.

**KIDS SEE GHOSTS** is the eponymous debut studio album by American hip hop super-duo Kids See Ghosts, composed of _Kanye West_ and _Kid Cudi_.
Released on June 8, 2018, by G.O.O.D. Music and Def Jam Recordings, the album is a deeply personal and introspective
exploration of _mental health_, _addiction_, and _spiritual awakening_.

The album, produced primarily by West and Cudi, features a diverse sound palette that blends elements of **hip hop**,
**psychedelic**, and **rock music**. Its seven tracks are concise yet _impactful_, with each song contributing to a cohesive narrative.
Critics lauded the album for its raw honesty, innovative production, and the palpable chemistry between West and Cudi.
It debuted at number two on the US Billboard 200 and was widely regarded as one of the best albums of 2018.

Oh, by the way, the best song in the entire album is **REBORN**.

## Trivia

*   **Wyoming Sessions:** The album was recorded in Wyoming during the same period as Kanye West's *Ye* and Pusha T's *Daytona*, contributing to what became known as the "Wyoming Sessions."
*   **Album Art:** The album's distinctive cover art was designed by contemporary Japanese artist [Takashi Murakami](/#/vocab/murakami).
*   **Critical Acclaim:** *KIDS SEE GHOSTS* received widespread critical acclaim, with many praising its unique sound and emotional depth.
