Chronometer of Ash

Tool / Artifact

A brass sphere powered by the ash of Glareach. It allows the user to relive exactly one hour of their past. The glass is unbreakable, ensuring the user can only observe, never intervene.

A navy blue notebook with a single silver star on the cover. It contains the observations of a diplomat who sees too much, documenting the rot at the heart of Thornus. Recently stolen by Nex Orlong outside the Kosnak Estate.

Touch of Rose

Disease / Mark

A devastating affliction that manifests as a crimson rose-leaf mark on the forehead. It slowly drains the victim's motor functions and speech, eventually leaving them in a vegetative state. Its only known cure is a rare drop of Alilberry.

Ancient Bio-Catalyst

An iridescent, vibrating fluid harvested from deep pockets within Proxan Mountain. Used by Proxanians for communal neural links. When pressurized with Viyu gas, it becomes a violent crimson 'Agitated' state.

Crimson Binder

Stabilizer / Pathogen

A viscous red concentrate used by Proxanians to bridge biological neural networks. Willare Frindjen mistakenly added this to the Krones Formula thinking it was nitrogen. Avalia realized this too late, knowing that the binder is now searching for a neural host in humans.

Dormant seeds of extinct flora that grew on Proxan Mountain. They respond only to the 'Proxanian Echo' and were the true foundation of the Krones Formula.

The background psychic frequency of the extinct Children of the Mountain. Avalia Krones sought to harness this for her 'botanical network', but it is now the phantom signal that the 'Touch of Rose' victims are biologically trying to connect to.
